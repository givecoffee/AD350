Get to Know Me

01

What's your name, and is there a particular nickname you prefer to go by?

+

Rachel, but I prefer to go by Rae as it is what my family and friends call me.

02

What's something interesting or unique about your background or family?

+

I grew up on a small farm in rural Tennessee. I also lived in different cities, from Nashville to San Diego. I currently live in the Seattle area.

My father and uncle were big on bluegrass and we were always playing something - whether it was a harmonica, banjo, steel drums, etc. I started playing mandolin as a kid, and now I split my time between guitar and uke.

03

Do you have any hobbies or interests that you're really passionate about?

+

I love playing stringed instruments, gardening, exploring, learning languages, and cooking. I am always looking for new ways to combine these interests in creative projects.

04

What do you hope to get out of this course?

+

I hope to learn best practices and make better architectural and backend design decisions.

05

What's the last book you read or movie you watched that you enjoyed?

+

The last movie I saw was last week, and it was called Project Hail Mary. I highly recommend it, especially if you like space movies.

06

Are you a morning person or a night owl, and why?

+

I am a little bit of both. From growing up on a farm, I naturally wake up at 5:30am without an alarm clock but if there are work shifts or other plans it can shift into me being more of a night owl since that is when I can have time alone to do classwork.

07

f you could travel anywhere in the world, where would you go, and why?

+

I would visit Japan, Australia, and Ireland. These places have always fascinated me from a cultural and historical perspective. Australia has a unique ecosystem and rich indigenous culture as well.

08

What's your favorite type of music, and do you have a favorite band or artist?

+

I love all types of music from RNB, Soul, to Alternative Rock. My favorite artist of all time would probably be Rihanna, and also a big fan of bands/artists like Alabama Shakes, Death Cab for Cutie, Jorja Smith, etc. I also grew up listening to a lot of Chris Cornell (Audioslave, Soundgarden, etc.) but I will also never skip a song by Curtis Mayfield or Donny Hathaway.

09

What's your go-to comfort food or favorite meal?

+

Nashville Hot Chicken (Hattie B’s recipe) with ideally waffles + hot honey.

10

Are you a cat person, a dog person, or neither?

+

Both! If I have a yard or neighborhood that is good for walking around, I prefer having a dog. If I have a busy lifestyle and need a more independent pet, cats are great for that.
